Laboratory Services

At Medicaid Radio-diagnostics, we offer our clients the best in Medical laboratory services.

Our laboratory process at Medicaid Radio-diagnostics involves the comparison of normal human structure and function to that of abnormal structure and function we identify during our various procedures. All samples collected from our clients are thoroughly examined so the right diagnosis can be made. Our state-of- the-art equipment aid in ensuring that the results of client examinations are accurate and received in record time.

Radiology Services

Have you ever thought about the technology that diagnostic imaging centers use?

Radiology is the area of medicine that uses X-rays, radioactive tracers, magnetic waves and ultrasonic waves to obtain detailed images of the inside of the body.

We have several imaging tests at Medicaid Radio-diagnostics

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Computer Tomography (CT)
  • Ultrasound Scan

Cancer Screening Service

Cancer screening is essential for your health.

MRD provides cancer screening to help you prevent and detect cancer early when it’s most treatable.The purpose of cancer screening is to:

  • Reduce the number of people who die from the disease, or prevent deaths from cancer altogether
  • Reduce the number of people who develop the disease
