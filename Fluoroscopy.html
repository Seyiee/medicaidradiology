Fluoroscopy is the dynamic evaluation of the body using x-rays. Fluoroscopy can be used for diagnostic procedures or to guide treatment. Fluoroscopy uses a continuous low-dose X-ray beam, often combined with a contrast material (dye), to produce images of organs and bones in real time. MRD radiologists have years of collective experience performing fluoroscopic procedures. When it comes to performing and interpreting fluoroscopic procedures experience matters. We perform a wide array of fluoroscopic procedures, including:

  • Fertility examinations (Hysterosalpingogram – HSG)
  • GI studies
  • Arthrograms

MRD has experienced Radiographers to perform your x-rays and experienced radiologists to interpret them.
