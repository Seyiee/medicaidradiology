OUR SERVICES

Laboratory Service

Our laboratory has some of the most advanced equipment available, including a state-of-the-art molecular laboratory for molecular diagnostic tests. Our highly trained and well experienced laboratory scientists are available to attend to your diagnostic needs.

Radiology

Radiology is the area of medicine that uses X-rays, radioactive tracers, magnetic waves and ultrasonic waves to obtain detailed images of the inside of the body.

MRI

MRI Scan (magnetic resonance imaging) is a painless technique that allows doctors to look at the soft tissues of the body. MRI scans are used to gather important information about your brain, spine, joints or other internal organs. Schedule an appointment for an MRI scan at one of our offices.

Computed Tomography

Computed tomography (CT) scans use X-rays and computer systems to create detailed images of many structures inside the body. CT scans show bones and soft tissues inside the body. CT scans are extremely valuable to doctors needing to make decisions very quickly. Visit Medicaid Radio-Diagnostics & Clinics to get an accurate CT exam.

Fluoroscopy

Fluoroscopy uses a continuous low-dose X-ray beam, often combined with a contrast material (dye) to produce images of organs and bones in real time. Our trained radiologists and technologists provide detailed fluoroscopy imaging services that are of substantial diagnostic benefits to you

Digital Mammography

Mammography uses a low dose X-ray to look for early signs of breast cancer, such as lumps that are too small to be felt either by yourself or your healthcare provider. Screening mammography is the only method proven to reduce deaths due to breast cancer by detecting breast cancer early.

Schedule your mammogram exam at Medicaid Radio Diagnostics & Clinics

Digital X-Ray

X-rays help doctors diagnose, treat and monitor a number of different medical conditions. Digital X-ray equipment provides superior image quality at lower radiation doses. We are proud to be your source for high-quality X-ray imaging services.

Ultrasound

Ultrasound imaging, also known as sonography, is a safe imaging technique that produces images of the inside of the body using high-frequency sound waves as opposed to radiation. Ultrasound scans can be used to help physicians diagnose a huge variety of medical conditions and come up with treatment plans. Visit our centre and benefit from our superior ultrasound imaging services.

Adult & Infant Vaccinations

Immunisation is a simple and effective way to stay protected from serious diseases. It not only helps protect individuals, it also protects the broader community by minimising the spread of disease. Adult and infant vaccines are available at Medicaid Radio-Diagnostics & Clinics

Special Consultation

We offer special consultation to our clients to know their pains and concerns and also to identify the reasons for their ill health.

Echo-cardiogram

An echocardiogram is a test that uses ultrasound to show how your heart muscle and valves are working. It is commonly known as 'Echo'

Endoscopy

Endoscopy is used in diagnosis, most commonly by performing a biopsy to check for conditions such as anemia, bleeding, inflammation, and cancers of the digestive system.

Wellness Clinic

Our wellness centre offers, colonic hydrotherapy, vitamin infusions and other holistic treatments that are of immense benefits to your immune system and gut health. Give your body the care and nourishment it deserves, book a session with us.

Electrocardiogram

An electrocardiogram is a simple, painless test that measures your heart’s electrical activity. It is also known as an 'ECG'

PCR Services Covid-19

PCR tests are used to directly detect the presence of an antigen, rather than the presence of the body’s immune response, or antibodies.

Domestic Staff Medical Screening

Our Domestic Staff Screening tests provide you with the necessary information you require to ensure that they don’t pose health risks to you and your loved ones.

Pre-marital Medical Screening

Pre-employment medical Screening

Our experienced team has caring hands to look after your employees needs and you can always depend on the efficient service for your need.

Health Screening Services

Home Care Service

Your aging ones are in safe hands. We provide first class services to your loved ones at home.

Ambulance Services

Cancer screening service

Cancer screening is essential for your health.MRD provides cancer screening to help you prevent and detect cancer early when it’s most treatable.

Telemedicine Services
